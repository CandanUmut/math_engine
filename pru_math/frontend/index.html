    Solve

    SymPy · LaTeX · natural language. ⌘/Ctrl + Enter submits.
    Intent

    SymPy syntax

    • x**2 + 3*x - 4 — expressions, ** for power
    • Eq(x**2 - 5*x + 6, 0) — equation
    • Integral(f, (x, a, b)) — definite
    • Integral(f, x) — indefinite
    • Derivative(f, x)
    • Limit(f, x, point)
    • Constants: pi, E, I, oo (∞)
    • Functions: sin cos tan exp log sqrt

    LaTeX (must contain a backslash)

    • \int_{0}^{1} x^2 dx
    • \sin(x)^2 + \cos(x)^2
    • \frac{d}{dx} x^3
    • \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}

    Natural language (Ollama)

    • "what is the integral of x squared from 0 to 1"
    • "differentiate sin(x) times x"
    • "factor x squared minus four"
    • ~5–30s via the local model

    Intent buttons

    • auto — engine decides (good for most inputs)
    • factor / expand / simplify — force the dispatch when input is a bare expression like x**4 - 16
    • solve — wraps your input as an equation
